About Species

The Anyrii are spirits that possess humanoids' bodies to live. These spirits can be seen through their crude physical manifestations via the host’s eyes. the Anyrii operate in a symbiotic or parasitic relationship depending on its compatibility with the host.

The Anyrii prefer humans as their host as they have the most malleable body types for them. It’s easier to learn communication among other skills with a human host than it is for other sorts of species hosts. There are generally two types of relationships that the Anyrii and Host fall under which have their pros and cons depending on which perspective you're looking from.

Parasitic Relationship - Conflicting relationships with the host result in a more bizarre and often distracting outward appearance. While they aren’t severely damaging to the host or the Anyrii, it isn’t good for surviving. Regular sickness is more prevalent and at most, there are serious complications of the host's body from gaining diseases that would otherwise be healed or blocked by the Anyrii.

Amalgamation Anyrii tend to have a Parasitic relationship.

Symbiotic Relationship - The more similar they are personality-wise, the more harmonious their appearance is. They often share memories, skills, and much more. The host's lifespan also benefits from the symbiotic relationship. However, if the Anyrii leaves a very old host, the physical body of the host will deteriorate quickly.

Standard Anyrii tend to have a Symbiotic relationship.

About Anyrii

The true origins of the Anyrii are unclear, whether it is derived from The Other or from the consciousness of humanity not even the oldest of the surviving Anyrii are sure. What is known is how Anyrii are formed nowadays. When older Anyrii create wisps, over time the wisps gain sentience separate from the Anyrii and mold themselves according to their wishes, however, if they wish to maintain this sentience they require a tangible body that anchors them into the physical realm. This is where the host comes in.
